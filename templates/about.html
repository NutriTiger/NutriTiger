About NutriTiger

NutriTiger is a web application that makes it easy for Princeton students who eat in the dining halls to keep track of their meals across all campus dining halls. Not only can it show what each dining hall is offering each day, but it will provide an in-depth breakdown of each menu item's macronutrients and allow users to build healthy habits and track their meals and nutrition statistics. Our goal is to provide a user-friendly platform to increase transparency between students and Campus Dining, whilst providing a resource to track food intake. NutriTiger was created by Oyu Enkhbold, Rishabh Jain (lead), Eric Kurkowski, Jewel Merriman, and Stephanie Oh.

Disclaimer

Our app is designed to promote healthy eating habits and nutritional awareness. NutriTiger & Campus Dining are committed to providing allergen & food preference information to the Princeton University community. Nutritive values are approximate, based on manufacturer data and an interface with the USDA Food and Nutrient Database. For specific questions regarding our recipes, reach out to Campus Dining's chefs and dietitians. If you are struggling with or have concerns about an eating disorder, we encourage you to seek guidance from a qualified healthcare professional who can offer customized assistance and support tailored to your individual circumstances.
